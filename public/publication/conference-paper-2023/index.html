Social Support Buffers Stress Effects on Mental Health: Evidence from Daily Diary Study

Your Name
June, 2023
Poster

Abstract

We conducted a 30-day daily diary study with 200 participants to examine how social support moderates the relationship between daily stressors and mental health outcomes. Results showed that perceived social support significantly buffered the negative effects of stress on mood and anxiety symptoms, particularly for individuals high in neuroticism.
